Referee report

Article ID:A/255635/PAP
Title:On the location of the surface-attached globule phase in collapsing polymers.
 
Second referee's report


The article contains a report on new results of the statistical mechanics
of polymers under competing conditions : the collapse transition and the
adsorption transition.
The critical properties of this problem are of great interest, but
the physics is not well understood.

The authors present new results, which are very important for the progress
of our understanding of this polymer problem.

The paper is very well written, the results are very well explained.
The length of the paper is adequate.

I recommend publication of the paper as is.
