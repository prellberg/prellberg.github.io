Dinosaur Crocodile
By Alexandra Prellberg
January 3, 2002 (age 4 years one week)

One day the crocodile was looking for a dinosaur. It was a tyrannosaurus Rex. It was so sad that it couldn't even find one because it wanted to have a baby. So it found something - one tyrannosaurus rex - and it said "I'm going to marry the crocodile so I guess I will do it then."

So it went and said "Hello can I marry you" and it said "sure because I think you are a nice crocodile. We can marry each other because I want to have a baby." And so they did it.

So the tyrannosaurus rex was the mommy and the crocodile was the papa and they wanted to have a baby duck and a baby hen and a baby frog and the crocodile didn't want to eat it or scare it. So then they colored stars or hearts or pictures of themselves.

The End
